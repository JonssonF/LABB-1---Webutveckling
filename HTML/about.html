About

Who am i?

I live in a house in Tvååker with my partner, Therese, whom I met in Lysekil while working as a head chef at Norra Hamnen 5.

I graduated from Peder Skrivares School in 2009, where I studied the Hotel & Restaurant program. After graduating, I worked seasonal jobs in Sälen at Högfjällshotellet during the winter and at Hotell Skansen in Båstad in the summer.

In 2012, I started working at Norra Hamnen 5 in Lysekil, where I took on various roles, including chef, sous-chef, and head chef.

In 2017, I moved back to my roots in Tvååker, where I worked at a restaurant called Prästgatan for about two years before changing careers to become a carpenter.

After earning my trade certification as a carpenter, I faced some job shortages, so I decided to improve my high school grades and pursue a degree in System Development at Campus Varberg, where I am currently studying.

What I Enjoy Doing

In my spare time, I love spending time with my kids, concerts, at my computer, hanging out with friends, cooking, and working on renovations or new building projects around the house.

I also enjoy traveling and never say no to an opportunity to get on a snowboard for an adventure!
